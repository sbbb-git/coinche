The 10 de der (last-trick bonus)

The 10 de der is the 10-point bonus awarded to the team that wins the last trick of the deal.

Where do these 10 points come from?

The cards in a deal are worth 152 points in total. You add 10 “de der” points (from dernier, “last”) for winning the final trick, bringing a deal's total to 162 points.

Why it matters

Those 10 points often decide a tight contract. Keeping a master trump or a winning card for the last trick is a real skill — “keeping the der.” Forgetting it can sink a contract you thought was won.

FAQ

What does “10 de der” mean?

It's a 10-point bonus given to the team that wins the last trick of the deal. “Der” is short for “dernier” (last).

Why is a deal worth 162 points?

The cards are worth 152 points, plus the 10-point last-trick bonus, giving 162 in total.
